This and the following method creates a fieldorder_wrapper object whose pointer points to the start of the memory chunk corresponds to the matrix at d, parity, x, row, col. These methods (as well as other wrap, wrap_ghost, wrap_index) are only available for the AoS orders, such as the QUDA_MILC_GAUGE_ORDER order, for the reason that the concept of memory chunk only applyies to these orders.
