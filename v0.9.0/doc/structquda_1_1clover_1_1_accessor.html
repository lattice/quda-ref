For each chirality the first 6 entires are the pure real diagonal entries. The following 30 entries correspond to the 15 complex numbers on the strictly lower triangular.

E.g., N = 6 (2 spins x 3 colors) and

entries = 1/2 * N * (N-1)

The storage order on the strictly lower triangular is column major, which complicates the indexing, since we have to count backwards from the end of the array.

The linear offset (in bottom-right coordinates) to the required element is simply 1/2*col'*(col'-1) + col - row.
